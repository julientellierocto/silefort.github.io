Every week, I try to read a few articles about tech, productivity, finances... and I summarize what I found relevant here:

  • Trunk Based Development: This one is certainly not new but I guess it took me some time to find it. I came to Trunk Based Development principles while reading the "Accelerate" book ( please read it, it is amaizing ! ). Trunk Based Dev is a framework, mindset, call it whatever you want that promote the use of as short lived branches as possible. This website is a introduction to the concept and why it should be used | https://trunkbaseddevelopment.com/
